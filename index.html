Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be Africa's most impactful, resilient, and innovative group—building legacy brands rooted in local stories and global ambition.

Mission

We empower African-born brands and communities by fusing culture, commerce, and creativity. We bring township energy, world-class innovation, and Ubuntu to every partner and project.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a multi-division powerhouse

2013

One man, a big dream, and the first campaign—born in the township.

2014–16

First taxi media. National clients. Built trust with brands like Nando's & Engen.

2017–19

Expansion. Disney Africa's first mall campaign. MBA studies (UCT GSB).

2020

Market disruption. Pandemic pause. Strategic reset.

2021–Today

Rebuilt, reimagined. Multi-division powerhouse. 10+ brands. 1.2M+ shoppers/month reached.

Our Philosophy

From AD.IT Solutions to TenOne Venture Group - a story of innovation, resilience, and African entrepreneurship

The Beginning

TenOne Venture Group started its journey in 2012 as AD.IT Solutions, igniting innovation in eThekwini with AD.IT TAXI TV and outdoor media.

Evolution

From those early days, we've grown into a storytelling-driven media and marketing company, driven by our belief that every brand has a unique story.

Philosophy

Inspired by "YiShumi OKunye. OKunye YiShumi" (You can't have ten without one), we believe that each person, effort, and story contributes to the whole.

Vision

As a 100% black youth-owned company, we stand as a testament to the power of representation, diversity, and entrepreneurship.
